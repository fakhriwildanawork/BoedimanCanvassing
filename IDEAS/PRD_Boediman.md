# Boediman ERP Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Goal
Mengintegrasikan seluruh proses bisnis Boediman Kopi.

- CRUD Supplier
- CRUD Mitra
- CRUD Gudang

- Purchase Request
- Purchase Order
- Goods Receipt

### Inventory
- Stock Card
- Batch
- Expired Date
- Stock Opname

### BOM
- Resep Produk
- Perhitungan HPP

### Production
- Production Order
- Material Consumption
- Finished Goods

### Distribution
- Transfer Gudang
- Delivery

### Selling
- POS
- Sales Order

### Canvassing
- Load
- Sell
- Return
- Waste

### Waste & Reject
- Expired
- Damage
- Return

### Finance
- Cash In
- Cash Out
- AP/AR
- Profit & Loss

## Acceptance Criteria
Seluruh transaksi harus memiliki audit trail dan role-based access.
